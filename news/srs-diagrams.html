1. Background

These diagrams are produced from the the comprehensive version of SRS published by Team M1 on April 5, 2026. That document integrates independent requirements analyses from S2 (UC-01 through UC-07), V1 (UC-V1-01 through UC-V1-03, UC-05), and M2 (UC-06, UC-07, patient/doctor/administrator interface use cases) into a single system-level SRS covering all six development teams.

Before producing the diagrams, the following minor corrections were applied based on the findings from our earlier SRS Error Review:

  • UC-03 trigger corrected from "Start Session" to "Connect Sensor" (was conflated with UC-04)
  • "Doctor" unified to "Medical Professional" in actor definitions for naming consistency
  • "System" and "Sensor Device" reclassified from Actors to System Components per UML conventions
  • UC-04 and UC-05 Actors fields: "System" removed, only external actors retained
  • UC-01 now includes email format validation (RFC 5322) as an explicit step
  • UC-03 notes WiFi connection path as pending specification alongside Bluetooth

2. Use Case Diagram

The diagram shows all actors, use cases, and their relationships across the system. Actors are positioned outside the system boundary; use cases are grouped by subsystem package.
