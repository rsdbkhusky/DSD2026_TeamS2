Sprint 1 Commitments

What S2 Must Deliver Before Sprint 1 Closes

Two deliverables from S2 are prerequisites for the rest of the project to move forward. They are not optional and cannot slip.

Mock Sensor Data Module

In Sprint 1

A working software mock that generates IF1-compliant data streams. Required by V1, V2, M1, and M2 before they can begin their own Sprint 1 work.

IF1 Contract — Frozen

In Sprint 1

The Sensor → Server interface contract, co-authored with V1, must be agreed and frozen by end of Sprint 1. All S2 and V1 code is built against this fixed specification.

Why the deadline is hard: The DSD project structure requires parallel development across all six teams. Without a frozen IF1 and a working mock module from S2, Teams V1, V2, M1, and M2 have no agreed data contract and no data to test against — which means they cannot make meaningful progress. S2's Sprint 1 is the system's critical path.
